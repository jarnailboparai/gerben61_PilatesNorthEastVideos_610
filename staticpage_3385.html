Carol and Gillian throughout their careers (too many years to mention!!), have worked extensively within the fitness environment. From both establishing successful studios lectures and teacher training in all aspects of Health and Fitness bring together a vast wealth of knowledge and expertise.

Gillian and Carol are passionate about their work and share the belief that practising Pilates can improve your lifestyle, understanding that each person has individual needs.

Pilates is unique in its delivery and especially at Pilates North East. Working together they deliever a ‘Dual Teach’ which results in a very high standard of teaching, ensuring clients are assessed, re-evaluated ensuring development and continued progression.
